Get a list of devices that can be typed into current packed-group, including devices that have been added and that have not been added.

Note: When checked in GroupDeviceBean is true, it means the device has been added to the combined group, and false is the device to be added to the combined group.

areaIdArea id
limitNumber of devices requested per page
